Our Environmental Commitments

Renewable Energy

Transitioning to 100% renewable energy sources in our manufacturing facilities by 2030, including solar and wind power.

Zero Waste Initiative

Implementing comprehensive recycling programs and working toward zero landfill waste in all our production processes.

Water Stewardship

Advanced water recycling systems that reduce consumption by 75% and ensure responsible water management.

Sustainable Packaging

Using eco-friendly, biodegradable materials for all product packaging, minimizing plastic use and environmental impact.

Responsible Sourcing

Partnering with local farmers who practice sustainable dairy production, ensuring ethical and environmentally-friendly supply chains.

Carbon Neutrality

Committed to achieving carbon neutrality by 2035 through emission reduction and carbon offset programs.
